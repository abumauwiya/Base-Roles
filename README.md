## Overview

**Base** is Coinbase’s optimistic rollup built on the OP Stack. It aims to bring the next billion users onchain by offering **secure, low-cost, and highly accessible** DeFi experiences.

This repository explains **why Base is becoming one of the strongest contenders in DeFi** compared to Ethereum mainnet, other Layer 2s, and Layer 1 alternatives like Solana.

## Why Base Matters in DeFi

Base combines the best of both worlds:
- Ethereum’s **security and developer ecosystem**
- Solana-like **low fees and speed**
- Coinbase’s **massive user distribution**

This unique positioning makes DeFi accessible not just to crypto natives, but to everyday users.

---

## Key Differentiators

✅ **Extremely Low Transaction Fees** – Often under $0.01–$0.05  
✅ **Full EVM Compatibility** – Deploy Ethereum contracts with minimal changes  
✅ **Coinbase Onboarding Superpower** – Easy access for 110M+ Coinbase users  
✅ **Ethereum Security** – Data is secured and settled on Ethereum L1  
✅ **High Throughput** – Hundreds of transactions per second  
✅ **Strong Builder Support** – Grants, ecosystem funds, and retroactive incentives  
✅ **Rapid Ecosystem Growth** – Leading DEXs, lending protocols, and yield opportunities
